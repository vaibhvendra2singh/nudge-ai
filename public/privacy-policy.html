Nudge

AI-Powered Task Management
Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: June 24, 2026  ·  Effective immediately
The short version: Nudge stores your task data only in your own browser. We never see it, never send it to a server, and never sell it. The only external service that receives any of your content is Google Gemini — and only when you explicitly trigger an AI feature.

1. Who We Are

Nudge ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered productivity application that helps you organize, prioritize, and act on tasks before deadlines. The Service is accessible at its hosted web address.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information stored locally in your browser

Nudge is a local-first application. The following information is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage — on your own device, and never transmitted to our servers during normal use:

  • Task titles, descriptions, and deadlines you create
  • Subtask and checklist items (including AI-generated ones you choose to keep)
  • Task completion status and priority settings
  • Your display name, if you set one in Settings
  • A randomly generated anonymous identifier (e.g. user-abc123xyz) used to keep your data separate if multiple people use the same device

We have no access to this data. It never leaves your browser unless you explicitly export it or trigger an AI feature.

2b. Information sent to AI when you use AI features

When you trigger any of the following AI-powered features, the relevant task content is sent from your browser to our backend server, which then forwards it to Google Gemini to generate a response:

  • AI Breakdown — your task title and description are sent to generate actionable subtasks
  • Nudge Message — your task title, deadline, and priority are sent to generate a motivational message
  • Needs Action Summary — titles and deadlines of your urgent tasks are sent to produce a single prioritization sentence
  • Voice Task Extraction — the text transcribed from your speech is sent for AI parsing into structured task fields
  • Predictive Analytics — anonymized task pattern data (completion rates, day-of-week statistics) is sent to identify behavioral risks

This content passes through our backend server solely to protect your API credentials. We do not log, store, or retain your task content on our servers after the AI response is returned to your browser.

2c. Google Calendar data (if you connect)

If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, we request read-only access using the calendar.readonly OAuth scope. We use this solely to display your upcoming events within the app for scheduling conflict detection. This data is used in-session in your browser only and is never stored on our servers.

2d. Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies
  • We do not use third-party analytics services
  • We do not collect payment information — Nudge is free to use
  • We do not run advertisements of any kind

3. How We Use Your Information

Task content sent to our backend server is used for one purpose only: to generate an AI response from Google Gemini and return it to your browser. We do not:

  • Store your task content on our servers
  • Use your content to train or fine-tune AI models
  • Share your content with any party other than Google Gemini, which is required to provide the AI features you request
  • Use your content for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose

4. Third-Party Services

Google Gemini (Google LLC)

We use the Gemini AI model via Google's API to power AI features in the app. When you trigger an AI feature, the relevant task content is processed by Google's servers under their terms and privacy policy. You can review Google's data practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Calendar API (optional)

If you connect Google Calendar, authentication is handled by Google OAuth. We only request read-only access and do not store calendar event data. You can revoke this access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

Google Cloud (infrastructure)

Our backend server is hosted on Google Cloud Run. Standard infrastructure access logs — including IP address, request timestamp, HTTP method, and response status — may be retained by Google Cloud for up to 30 days per their standard infrastructure policies. We do not access or use these logs for any application-level purpose.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your task data lives in localStorage and persists on your device until you:

  • Use the "Wipe Database Cache" option in the Settings page to clear all tasks
  • Clear your browser's site data or local storage through your browser settings
  • Uninstall or reset your browser

Because we never receive your task data, we have no ability to delete it on your behalf. You are in full control of your data at all times.

6. Data Security

Your task data does not leave your device during normal use. When AI features are triggered, data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption) to our backend server, processed in memory to construct the Gemini API request, and the response is immediately returned to your browser. No task content is written to disk or a database on our servers. API credentials are stored as server-side environment variables and are never exposed to your browser.

7. Children's Privacy

Nudge is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used this Service in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us using the details below.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no data held by us to request, correct, or delete on your behalf. All your task data is under your direct control within your browser. You may export, modify, or permanently delete it at any time through the app's Settings page or your browser's developer tools.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, UK, or another jurisdiction with data protection laws, and believe we have processed personal data about you, please contact us and we will respond as required by applicable law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
